Experience realistic emergency services role-play in the German city of Hamburg

About Emergency Hamburg

Emergency Hamburg is an emergency services role-playing game on the Roblox platform, where you can be a police officer, citizen, bus driver, car mechanic, truck driver, firefighter, medic, or criminal in the German city Hamburg.

Play on Roblox Now

Available Roles

Police Officer

Maintain law and order in Hamburg

Firefighter

Respond to emergency fire situations

Medic

Provide medical assistance to citizens

Citizen

Live your daily life in Hamburg

Bus Driver

Transport citizens around the city

Car Mechanic

Repair and maintain vehicles

Truck Driver

Deliver goods across Hamburg

Criminal

Challenge the law enforcement
